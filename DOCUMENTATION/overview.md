****Description:****

`WiFixES` (Wi-Fi Evaluation System) is a `terminal-based`, `passive Wi-Fi intelligence` and `evaluation tool`.  
It analyzes wireless environments for signal strength, stability, congestion, and potential risks **without performing any attacks**.  

****Purpose:****

- Academic & research projects  
- Wireless environment analysis  
- Cybersecurity learning `(ethical & passive)`
- Network health evaluation  

****Key Features:****
- Passive Wi-Fi scanning `(SSID, signal strength, channel, security type)`
- Stability Index & Environment Health Score  
- Risk Level Classification `(LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH)`
- Scan history & comparison  
- Professional terminal-based interface  
- Legal & ethical compliance  

****Target Users:****

- College students & researchers  
- Networking and cybersecurity learners  
- Ethical hacking demonstrations
